## ‚ú® Features

### üíß Mana System
- Every player has a **Mana Pool** (default 100).
- Mana regenerates naturally over time.
- Stand near a **Mana Altar** to regenerate mana faster!
- Mana is displayed in a custom HUD overlay.

### ü™Ñ Wands & Progression
There are 4 tiers of wands, each with better stats:

| Wand Tier | Slots | Mana Capacity | Cooldown Reduction | Rarity |
|-----------|-------|---------------|-------------------|--------|
| **Apprentice** | 1 | Low | 0% | Common |
| **Adept** | 2 | Medium | 15% | Uncommon |
| **Master** | 3 | High | 30% | Rare |
| **Archmage** | 4 | Extreme | 50% | Epic |

*Right-click to cast, Sneak + Scroll to switch spells.*

### ‚ö° Spells
Learn spells by finding **Spell Tomes** and binding them to your wand:

- **üî• Fireball** - Launches an explosive fire projectile.
- **‚ùÑÔ∏è Ice Shard** - Fires a freezing shard that slows enemies.
- **‚ö° Lightning Bolt** - Summons chain lightning from the sky.
- **üíö Heal** - Restores health to the caster.
- **üåÄ Teleport** - Instantly teleports you forward.
- **‚òÑÔ∏è Meteor Shower** - (Archmage only) Rains destruction from the heavens.

### üß± Magical Blocks
- **Mana Altar** - Boosts mana regeneration for nearby players.
- **Arcane Pedestal** - Used for magical crafting and upgrading wands.
- **Magic Ore** - Found underground, drops Mana Crystal Shards.
- **Rune Stone** - Decorative block infused with magic.
